My Journey

Born on 15 October 1931,at Rameswaram in Tamil Nadu,Dr Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalamspecialized in aeronautical engineering from Madras Institute of Technology. Dr kalam is one of the most distinguished scientist of India and has recived honorary doctorates from forty-five universities and institutions in India and abroad.He has been awarded the Padma Bhushan(1981),the Padma Vibhushan(1990) and India highest civilian award, the Bharat Ratan (1997).He has also recived the King Charles II Medal (2007), the Woodrow Wilson award (2008), the Hoover award (2008) and the International Von Karman Wings Award (2009) among other International accolades.
Dr Kalam became the eleventh President of India on 25 July 2002.His focus and greatest ambition remains finding ways to transform India into a developed nation.
